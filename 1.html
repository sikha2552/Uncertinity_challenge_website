Motivation

Uncertainty and interrater variability pose significant challenges in the field of Deep Learning (DL) for medical image segmentation, and their relationship is closely intertwined.In medical imaging, DL models are often tasked with delineating structures or abnormalities within complex anatomical structures, such as tumors, blood vessels, or organs....

We will be studying three structures: liver, kidney, and pancreas. These three structures were chosen since they are complementary: liver, as a big organ containing ambiguous anatomical structures, such as blood vessels; kidney, a little bit harder to segment than the liver and a little bit more irregular; pancreas, the hardest of the three being the most heterogeneous and irregular. Furthermore, it is important to highlight that the different labels that will be used for the challenges are obtained from three different experts following a defined clinical guideline, so they can be comparable. They have been supervised to be sure that there are no discrepancies in how the organs must be segmented.
